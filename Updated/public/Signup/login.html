Instructions for exam


●Attempt ALL questions. For each correct answer, you will be awarded 1 mark and for each incorrect answer, 0.5 marks will be DEDUCTED from your total.

● DO NOT SWITCH to any other window during the test. If you do so, you will be logged out automatically and will be DISQUALIFIED from the exam.

● Also DO NOT press BACK button or RELOAD button during the test. Doing so will result in DIS-QUALIFICATION.

● Left Hand panel indicates the status of each question using a color code –
○ Attempted questions – Green
○ Not attempted questions – Grey
○ Flagged questions for review – Red flag over Green or Grey as the case may be

● Your data will be AUTO SAVED every 30 seconds. So, in the case of any system failure, the test can be resumed from where it was terminated.

● When you are ready to submit the test, press SAVE AND SUBMIT button.

● The paper will be automatically submitted as soon as the time runs out.

● The flag is just for the user's convenience and will NOT affect their score in the test.

● In case you faced any issue while taking test please contact hr@genesistechnologies.in with the details, mentioning your userid.
